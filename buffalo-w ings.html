Buffalo Chicken Wings

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs chicken wings
  • 1/2 cup hot sauce
  • 1/4 cup melted butter
  • 1 tablespoon vinegar
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Celery sticks and blue cheese dressing for serving

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. In a bowl, mix hot sauce, melted butter, vinegar, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
  3. Place chicken wings on a baking sheet and bake for 40-45 minutes, flipping halfway through.
  4. Toss the baked wings in the sauce mixture until well coated.
  5. Serve with celery sticks and blue cheese dressing.
