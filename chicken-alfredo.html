Chicken Alfredo

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 8 oz fettuccine pasta
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Cook fettuccine according to package instructions; drain.
  2. In a skillet, melt butter over medium heat. Add chicken and cook until golden and no longer pink inside.
  3. Remove chicken and slice it. In the same skillet, add heavy cream and bring to a simmer.
  4. Stir in Parmesan cheese until melted. Add chicken and pasta; toss to coat.
  5. Season with salt and pepper. Garnish with parsley before serving.
