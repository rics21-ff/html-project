Chicken Enchiladas

These chicken enchiladas are a comforting and flavorful dish, perfect for a family dinner!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked chicken, shredded
  • 1 cup enchilada sauce
  • 8 corn tortillas
  • 1 cup shredded cheese (cheddar or Mexican blend)
  • 1/2 cup diced onion
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a bowl, combine shredded chicken, half of the enchilada sauce, diced onion, salt, and pepper.
  3. Warm the tortillas in a skillet until pliable. Fill each tortilla with the chicken mixture, roll them up, and place seam-side down in a baking dish.
  4. Pour the remaining enchilada sauce over the rolled tortillas and sprinkle with cheese.
  5. Bake for 20-25 minutes or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  6. Garnish with chopped cilantro before serving.
