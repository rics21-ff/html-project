Chicken Fajitas

Ingredients: chicken breast, bell peppers, onion, tortillas, salsa

Instructions: Saute chicken breast and bell peppers in olive oil until tender. Serve with tortillas, salsa, and your favorite toppings.

Tips: You can add your favorite toppings such as sour cream, avocado, or shredded cheese. You can also serve with Mexican rice or beans.
