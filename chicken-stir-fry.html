Ingredients

  • 1 lb chicken breast, sliced
  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (bell peppers, broccoli, carrots)
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ginger, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Cooked rice for serving

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Add chicken and cook until browned and cooked through.
  3. Add garlic and ginger; saut√© for 1 minute.
  4. Add mixed vegetables and stir-fry until tender-crisp.
  5. Pour in soy sauce and toss to coat. Season with salt and pepper.
  6. Serve over cooked rice.
