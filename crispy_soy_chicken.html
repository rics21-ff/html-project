Crispy Soy Chicken

Servings: 4-6 people

INGREDIENTS:

  • 1 1/2 pounds boneless, skinless chicken breast or thighs, cut into 1-inch pieces
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup cornstarch
  • 1/2 cup panko breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon grated ginger
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Fresh scallions, for garnish

INSTRUCTIONS:

  1. In a shallow dish, mix together flour, cornstarch, and panko breadcrumbs.
  2. In a separate dish, whisk together soy sauce, olive oil, garlic, and ginger.
  3. Add the chicken to the soy sauce mixture and marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  4. Remove the chicken from the marinade and coat with the flour mixture.
  5. Heat about 1/2 inch of oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  6. Fry the chicken until crispy and golden brown, about 5-7 minutes.
  7. Drain on paper towels and season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Garnish with fresh scallions and serve hot.
