Honey Garlic Chicken

Ingredients

  • 4 chicken thighs, boneless and skinless
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped green onions for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix honey, soy sauce, garlic, olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  2. Marinate chicken in the mixture for at least 30 minutes.
  3. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C) and place the chicken in a baking dish.
  4. Pour the remaining marinade over the chicken and bake for 25-30 minutes.
  5. Garnish with chopped green onions before serving.
